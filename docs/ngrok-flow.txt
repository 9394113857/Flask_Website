Interview explanation:-
If an interviewer asks, "Why do we have a health endpoint?", you can answer:
"A health endpoint lets monitoring systems, load balancers, Kubernetes, or DevOps tools quickly verify that the application is running and responding. It's also useful during development to confirm the server is reachable before testing business APIs."
This is a common pattern in production services and a good practice to include even in smaller Flask applications.
